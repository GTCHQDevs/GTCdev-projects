Presiding Bishop

Kingsford Bloomfield

Bishop Kingsford A. Bloomfield, born in Passage Fort, St. Catherine, Jamaica, accepted the Lord at the tender age of nine and a half while attending The Rehoboth Apostolic Church. After witnessing his friend’s decision to accept Christ, he too, decided to follow the same path that very night. He went on to graduate from St. Jago High School and the University of the West Indies at Mona. As a young minister, he served under the leadership of Bishop B.S.E. Dyer primarily as a musician and a Sunday School teacher while ardently supporting the ministry in various other areas.

After migrating to the United States, he faithfully served under our late founder, Bishop Samuel Green II at the Headquarters church as an Elder, Musician, and Bible Teacher, among various other duties. In 1997 he was sent to the Franklin Ave branch and served as the Pastor there for seven years. Bishop, Mother Bloomfield and the saints there experienced a tremendous transformation and growth of the ministry there. While at the Franklin Ave branch, he was consecrated as an Overseer. After the death of our Founding Bishop in November 2003, he returned to pastor the Headquarters church in 2004. Subsequently in October 2006, he was consecrated as the Presiding Bishop of the Gospel Tabernacle churches. He currently serves in this capacity overseeing the 11 branches within the organization.

His wife of almost 45 years, Mother Janette Bloomfield, a powerful and committed woman of God is his biggest supporter who is in constant prayer and intercession on his behalf. They both have worked in ministry for most of their lives and continue to be steadfast in their dedication to serving the people of God. Together were blessed by the Lord with three children, Kijan, Kajette, and Kingsford II as well as many adopted and spiritual children. Bishop Bloomfield is fully surrendered to the call of God on his life and trusts God explicitly for direction. He often can be heard encouraging the saints to exercise their faith in God without reservation, this he believes will allow us to truly experience all God has for us individually and as a church family.

Pastor

Elder Kevin Gayle

Elder Kevin Gayle, a third-generation Apostolic preacher, was born in Manchester, Jamaica, WI and migrated with his family to Brooklyn, NY at the age of four. He spent 17 years under the leadership of the late, Apostle and Founder of the Gospel Tabernacle Church, Bishop Samuel Green II. During this time he accepted the Lord as his personal savior and was carefully nurtured in the gospel by his Pastor and the church family.

He currently serves as the Pastor of this ministry under the leadership of the current Presiding Bishop, Bishop Kingsford A. Bloomfield Sr.. Elder Gayle has served in various capacities in the ministry over the years. In particular, he has an intense desire to see youth enthused and fully engaged in the work of the Lord. This passion to see young people succeed has spilled over into his professional life, as he is employed as an Academic Counselor at the Kingsborough Community College. Elder Gayle holds a Bachelor's Degree in Health Science, a Master's Degree in Social Work, and a Master's Degree in Ministry. He is married to the one he calls, his "rib", Evangelist Sherlene Gayle; a powerful intercessor who stands by him all the way. God has blessed them with four wonderful children; Sarah, Khloe, Simon, and Nathan. He desires to do ALL that God has commissioned him to do and live that his life will bring glory to God in all things.

Senior Elder

Elder Richard Davis

Elder Richard Davis was born in Manchester, Jamaica, WI, the sixth of eight children. At age 12, he was baptized in Jesus’ name and later received the Holy Ghost at the Shiloh Church of Jesus Christ, Apostolic. In 2002, he was ordained by the late Bishop Evans of Waterloo, St. Elizabeth, and served faithfully until migrating to the U.S. with his family in 2006.

He now worships at the Gospel Tabernacle Church of Jesus Christ in Brooklyn, NY under the leadership of Bishop Kingsford Bloomfield. Known for his dedication to ministry and deep love for God’s Word, he was elevated to the office of Elder within the GTC organization. He holds a Doctorate in Theology from Mount Olive Theological Seminary and has served in tent ministry across the U.S. and Caribbean for over 15 years.

Elder Davis has been married to his beloved wife for over 30 years. Together they are blessed with three children. He remains committed to fulfilling God’s call with humility and unwavering devotion.

Elder

Elder Owen Wattson

Elder Owen Wattson born in Saint Elizabeth, Jamaica, gave his life to the Lord in 1998 after being drawn deeper into the Apostolic faith. His walk with Christ has been shaped by the examples of others, a love for learning, and a desire to grow through both encouragement and correction.

At our church he serves as an Elder, where he leads the Men’s Department, teaches Sunday School, and assists with baptisms. Elder Wattson believes God has called him to preach, teach, and most importantly live a life that draws others to Christ.

He is supported in ministry by his wife, a prayerful partner in faith, and together they are raising their daughter Victoria to know and love the Lord by example both at home and in the church.

Senior Minister

Dr Merlene Scott-Burrell

Minister Merlene Scott-Burrell was born in St. Ann, Jamaica. She holds a BS in Accounting and a Certificate in Management Studies from the University of the West Indies, Mona. She accepted Christ in 1978 at Pentecostal Gospel Temple under Bishop Carmen Stewart, where she served in the Church Office and Missions.

Since 1992, she has been a devoted member of the Gospel Tabernacle Church of Jesus Christ, inspired by the leadership of the late Bishop Samuel Green and Bishop Kingsford Bloomfield. She earned her Doctorate in Theology in 2002 and has served in roles including Church Treasurer, Chaplain, and Sunday School Superintendent.

A Certified Public Accountant and retired New York State employee, Minister Scott-Burrell continues to serve faithfully as Minister and Church Mother alongside Pastor Kevin Gayle. She is married to Conron Burrell and is blessed with two children and seven grandchildren. Her life is anchored in Psalm 34:1 — “I will bless the Lord at all times, His praise shall continually be in my mouth.”
